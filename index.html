Why Us

Why Choose Our Spices and Flours

01

Freshness Guaranteed

We deliver freshly powdered spices directly to the customer as soon as it is packed to retain the freshness. Subscribing to our deliveries ensures that we procure only when it is time for your delivery. That way freshness is guaranteed.

02

No chemicals or adulterants

Our entire facility is live streamed for public access. So from the moment ingredients arrive, to the processing till packaging can be monitored by you. Hence you can rest assured that no chemicals or adulterants are added to the food.

03

Blockchain verified

Every thing from procurement to processing to packing till delivery is logged via public blockchain ledger to ensure trust for our customers. Decentralised blockchain ensures complete transparency in all stages.
